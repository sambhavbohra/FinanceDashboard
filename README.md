# 🏛️ FinTrack: High-Integrity Democratic Financial Hub

**FinTrack** is a state-of-the-art communal financial governance system designed for high-integrity debt management, democratic consensus, and rapid squad dispersal. It re-imagines shared expenses not as unilateral entries, but as a **Democratic Ledger** where every communal modification requires the unanimous consent of the squad.

---

### 🏛️ Core Architectural Protocols

#### 1. The Democratic Consensus Engine (Communal Gatekeeper)
Every shared financial record is protected by the **Consensus Protocol**. 
-   **No Unilateral Edits**: Any modification or deletion of an expense involving real human members (excluding Offline Partners/Test Friends) is automatically frozen.
-   **Revision Requests**: A high-integrity **Revision Request** is broadcast to the **REVISED DECISIONS** hub of every involved member.
-   **Unanimous Approval**: Modifications only commit to the global ledger once every human member has provided explicit, high-integrity approval.

#### 2. The Squad Disperse Protocol (Fragmentation Mastery)
Bypass group bloat and manage complex squad bills via the **Disperse Engine**.
-   **Multi-Payer Support**: Record complex transactions where multiple squad members contributed (e.g., X paid half, Y paid half).
-   **1-on-1 Fragmentation**: The engine surgically fragment a single squad-wide bill into separate, detailed 1-on-1 private ledgers. 
-   **Net-Debt Integrity**: Balances are intelligently recalibrated across individual friend relationships to maintain a clean, group-less record of who owes whom.

#### 3. High-Integrity Mobile-First UX
-   **Surgical Navigation**: A mobile-optimized SPA architecture with pinned interaction hubs and scroll-contained modals.
-   **Vercel-Ready SPA Routing**: Implemented a global rewrite protocol to ensure production reloads on any sub-route are refresh-proof and friction-less.
-   **Touch-Optimized Toggles**: Rapid "Tick-to-Select" payer logic with auto-allocation for common single-payer scenarios.
